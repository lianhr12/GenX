## Quick Start in 4 Steps

<Steps>

### Create Your Account

Sign up at [GenX.art](https://genx.art) to get started. New users receive **50 free credits** immediately.

### Choose Your Creation Method

Select from Image to Video, Text to Video, or Reference Video based on your needs.

### Select an Art Style

Choose from 5 stunning styles:
- **Cyberpunk** - Neon-lit futuristic aesthetics
- **Watercolor** - Soft, flowing artistic effects
- **Oil Painting** - Rich, textured classical feel
- **Anime** - Japanese animation style
- **Fluid Art** - Mesmerizing liquid motion effects

### Generate and Download

Click generate, wait 15-60 seconds, and download your masterpiece in 1080p quality.

</Steps>

## Art Styles Overview

| Style | Best For | Mood |
|-------|----------|------|
| Cyberpunk | Tech, gaming, music | Futuristic, energetic |
| Watercolor | Nature, romance, calm | Soft, dreamy |
| Oil Painting | Portraits, dramatic | Classical, premium |
| Anime | Characters, stories | Dynamic, expressive |

Learn more about each style in our [Art Styles Guide](/docs/art-styles).

## Credit System

GenX uses a credit system for video generation:

| Video Duration | Credits Used |
|---------------|--------------|
| 3-5 seconds | 5 credits |
| 6-15 seconds | 10 credits |
| 16-30 seconds | 20 credits |

New users start with 50 free credits. Learn more about [Credits](/docs/account/credits) and [Pricing](/docs/account/pricing).
