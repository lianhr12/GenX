## General Questions

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="What is GenX?">
GenX.art is an AI-powered artistic video creation platform that transforms your images and text into stunning, stylized videos. We offer five artistic styles and three creation methods to bring your creative vision to life.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need any technical skills to use GenX?">
No! GenX is designed to be user-friendly. If you can upload an image or write a sentence, you can create beautiful videos. No video editing experience required.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What art styles are available?">
GenX offers five curated artistic styles:
- **Cyberpunk** - Futuristic, neon-lit aesthetics
- **Watercolor** - Soft, flowing artistic effects
- **Oil Painting** - Rich, textured classical feel
- **Anime** - Japanese animation style
- **Fluid Art** - Abstract liquid motion effects
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What video quality can I expect?">
All videos are generated in 1080p (1920×1080) resolution at 24-30 fps, delivered in MP4 format. This is broadcast-quality output suitable for any platform.
</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Account & Billing

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="How do I create an account?">
Visit [genx.art/register](/register) and sign up with your email, or use Google/GitHub for one-click registration. New accounts receive 15 free credits.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through Stripe. Enterprise customers can arrange invoicing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers who haven't used more than 100 credits. Contact support@genx.art to request a refund.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do unused credits roll over?">
Subscription credits expire at the end of each billing cycle and do not roll over. However, purchased credit packages never expire.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
Go to Settings → Billing and click "Cancel Subscription." Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Creation & Features

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="What's the difference between Image to Video and Text to Video?">
**Image to Video** transforms your existing images into animated videos. You provide the visual content, and AI adds motion and style.

**Text to Video** generates entirely new visual content from your text descriptions. No images needed—just describe what you want to see.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long can videos be?">
Videos can be 3-30 seconds long. Longer videos use more credits. We recommend starting with shorter videos to test your ideas.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What image formats are supported?">
We support JPG and PNG images up to 10MB each. For best results, use high-quality images at 1080p resolution or higher.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does generation take?">
Most videos generate in 15-60 seconds, depending on duration and complexity. You'll see real-time progress during generation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use my videos commercially?">
Yes! You own full rights to videos you create with GenX. Use them for personal projects, social media, marketing, or any commercial purpose.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a watermark on videos?">
Free tier videos include a small watermark. Pro subscribers get watermark-free downloads.
</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Credits & Pricing

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="How does the credit system work?">
Credits are consumed when you generate videos. Different durations use different amounts:
- 3-5 seconds: 5 credits
- 6-15 seconds: 10 credits
- 16-30 seconds: 20 credits

Reference Video uses slightly more credits due to additional processing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How many free credits do I get?">
New users receive 15 free credits upon registration. These credits expire after 30 days.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I run out of credits?">
You can purchase additional credit packages at any time, or upgrade to a Pro subscription for monthly credits.
</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Technical Questions

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="What browsers are supported?">
GenX works best on modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my content private?">
Yes. Your uploaded images and generated videos are private to your account. We don't share or use your content without permission.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you use my content to train AI?">
No. We do not use your uploaded content or generated videos to train our AI models without explicit consent.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if generation fails?">
If a generation fails, your credits are automatically refunded. You can try again or contact support if issues persist.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I download my videos later?">
Yes, all generated videos are saved to your account. You can download them anytime from your dashboard or gallery.
</Accordion>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="My video looks different than expected">
Try these solutions:
- Use higher quality source images
- Simplify your text prompts
- Try a different art style
- Adjust motion intensity settings
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Generation is taking too long">
Generation usually takes 15-60 seconds. If it's taking longer:
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if it exceeds 5 minutes
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I can't upload my image">
Ensure your image:
- Is JPG or PNG format
- Is under 10MB
- Has a standard aspect ratio
- Isn't corrupted
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My credits weren't refunded for a failed generation">
Credits should auto-refund for failures. If they weren't:
- Wait a few minutes and refresh
- Check your credit history
- Contact support@genx.art
</Accordion>
