## Managing Your Subscription

This guide covers everything you need to know about managing your GenX subscription, including upgrades, downgrades, and cancellations.

## Subscription Plans

GenX offers two tiers:

| Plan | Price | Credits | Billing |
|------|-------|---------|---------|
| Free | $0 | 15 (signup only) | N/A |
| Pro Monthly | $9.90/month | 800/month | Monthly |
| Pro Yearly | $99/year | 800/month | Annually |

## How to Subscribe

1. Go to [Pricing](/pricing)
2. Select your preferred plan
3. Click "Subscribe" or "Buy Now"
4. Complete payment through Stripe
5. Your subscription is activated immediately

## Managing Your Subscription

### Access Subscription Settings

Navigate to **Settings → Billing** to:
- View current plan
- See next billing date
- Update payment method
- Download invoices
- Manage subscription

### Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade:
1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Upgrade Plan"
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm and pay

When upgrading:
- Change takes effect immediately
- You're charged the prorated difference
- New credits are added to your balance

### Downgrading Your Plan

To downgrade:
1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select a lower tier
4. Confirm the change

When downgrading:
- Change takes effect at next billing cycle
- Current benefits continue until then
- No partial refunds for unused time

### Canceling Your Subscription

To cancel:
1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. (Optional) Provide feedback

After canceling:
- Access continues until billing period ends
- You'll be moved to the free tier
- Existing credits remain until they expire

## Payment Management

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Update Payment Method"
3. Enter new card details
4. Save changes

### Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
1. We'll retry automatically
2. You'll receive email notifications
3. Update your payment method if needed
4. Service continues for a grace period

### Invoices

Download invoices:
1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Billing History"
3. Download individual invoices

## Refund Policy

### 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

New subscribers can request a full refund within 14 days if:
- You haven't used more than 100 credits
- It's your first subscription
- You contact support within 14 days

### How to Request a Refund

1. Email support@genx.art
2. Include your account email
3. Provide reason for refund
4. Allow 5-7 business days for processing

### Non-Refundable Items

- Credit packages (after purchase)
- Subscriptions after 14 days
- Partial month/year refunds

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I pause my subscription?
Currently, we don't offer pause functionality. You can cancel and resubscribe when ready.

### What happens to my videos if I cancel?
Your generated videos remain accessible in your account. You can download them anytime.

### Can I switch between monthly and yearly billing?
Yes, you can switch at any time. The change takes effect at your next billing date.

### Do you offer student or nonprofit discounts?
Contact support@genx.art with verification for potential discounts.

### What happens if my card expires?
Update your payment method before expiration to avoid service interruption.
