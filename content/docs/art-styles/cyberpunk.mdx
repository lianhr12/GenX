## Neon Dreams of Tomorrow

The Cyberpunk style transforms your content into a vision of the digital future. Inspired by films like Blade Runner, Ghost in the Shell, and the neon-soaked aesthetics of synthwave culture.

## Visual Characteristics

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Electric blue, hot pink, vivid purple
- **Accent:** Neon green, orange, cyan
- **Base:** Deep black, dark gray, navy

### Key Features
- High contrast lighting
- Neon glow effects
- Glitch and digital artifacts
- Urban, dystopian atmosphere
- Reflective surfaces
- Light trails and lens flares

### Mood
- Futuristic
- Edgy
- High-tech
- Mysterious
- Energetic

## Best Use Cases

### Perfect For
- Tech product showcases
- Gaming content
- Music videos (especially electronic/synthwave)
- Sci-fi themed content
- Night scene transformations
- Brand content for tech companies
- Social media content targeting younger audiences

### Examples

| Content Type | Result |
|-------------|--------|
| City photos | Neon-lit urban landscapes |
| Portraits | Stylized with color lighting |
| Products | Futuristic presentation |
| Abstract | Digital, glitchy effects |

## Tips for Best Results

### Source Image Guidelines

**Ideal source images:**
- Urban environments and cityscapes
- Night scenes
- Images with clear subjects
- Photos with existing contrast
- Tech-related subjects

**Avoid:**
- Very bright, sunny images
- Natural landscapes (unless going for contrast)
- Images with lots of green (unless intentional)

### Prompt Tips (for Text to Video)

**Effective keywords:**
- "neon lights"
- "rain-soaked streets"
- "holographic"
- "digital"
- "futuristic city"
- "night scene"

**Example prompts:**
> "A busy intersection in a futuristic city at night, neon signs everywhere, rain reflecting on the streets"

> "A mysterious figure in a hoodie walking through a neon-lit alley, cyberpunk atmosphere"

### Settings Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Motion Intensity | Medium to High |
| Duration | 5-15 seconds works best |
| Best with | Urban images, portraits, tech content |

## Style Combinations

Cyberpunk pairs well with:
- **Fast-paced editing** for music videos
- **Slow, atmospheric movement** for mood pieces
- **Glitch transitions** for dynamic content

## Common Applications

### Gaming Content
Perfect for gaming channels, esports content, and game trailers. The style naturally fits gaming aesthetics.

### Music Videos
Ideal for electronic, synthwave, and hip-hop music videos. The dynamic visuals match high-energy music.

### Tech Marketing
Great for software demos, app previews, and tech product launches. Conveys innovation and modernity.

### Social Media
Eye-catching content that stands out in feeds. Works especially well on TikTok and Instagram Reels.

<Callout title="Pro Tip">
For maximum impact, use images with existing light sources (streetlights, screens, windows) - the AI will transform these into stunning neon elements.
</Callout>
