## Choosing the Right Style

### Consider Your Content

Different types of content work better with different styles:

**Tech & Gaming**
- Primary: Cyberpunk
- Alternative: Anime

**Nature & Landscapes**
- Primary: Watercolor
- Alternative: Oil Painting

**People & Portraits**
- Primary: Oil Painting
- Alternative: Anime

**Abstract & Artistic**
- Primary: Fluid Art
- Alternative: Watercolor

**Stories & Characters**
- Primary: Anime
- Alternative: Oil Painting

### Consider Your Audience

| Audience | Recommended Styles |
|----------|-------------------|
| Professional/Corporate | Oil Painting, Watercolor |
| Young/Trendy | Cyberpunk, Anime |
| Artistic/Creative | Fluid Art, Oil Painting |
| Family/General | Watercolor, Anime |

### Consider Your Platform

| Platform | Recommended Styles |
|----------|-------------------|
| Instagram | All styles work well |
| TikTok | Cyberpunk, Anime |
| YouTube | All styles work well |
| Corporate | Oil Painting, Watercolor |
| Portfolio | Fluid Art, Oil Painting |

## Style Tips

### Consistency Matters

For series or brand content, stick to one style for visual consistency. This helps build recognition and professional appearance.

### Experiment First

Before committing to a style for a big project, test with shorter videos to see how your content transforms.
