## Flowing Beauty

The Watercolor style brings the gentle elegance of traditional watercolor painting to your videos. Colors blend and flow organically, creating soft, dreamlike visuals.

## Visual Characteristics

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Soft pastels, translucent hues
- **Accent:** Gentle blues, pinks, greens
- **Base:** Light, airy backgrounds

### Key Features
- Soft color transitions
- Organic, flowing edges
- Translucent layering
- Natural, gentle movement
- Bleeding color effects
- Paper texture hints

### Mood
- Soft
- Dreamlike
- Romantic
- Peaceful
- Organic

## Best Use Cases

### Perfect For
- Nature and landscape content
- Wedding and romantic videos
- Children's content
- Meditation and wellness
- Soft brand content
- Artistic expressions
- Poetry and literary content

### Examples

| Content Type | Result |
|-------------|--------|
| Flowers | Delicate, blooming effects |
| Landscapes | Soft, painterly vistas |
| Portraits | Gentle, artistic rendering |
| Nature | Organic, flowing beauty |

## Tips for Best Results

### Source Image Guidelines

**Ideal source images:**
- Well-lit, soft lighting
- Nature subjects (flowers, trees, water)
- Gentle color palettes
- Clear, simple compositions
- Portrait photography

**Avoid:**
- High contrast images
- Very dark photos
- Busy, complex scenes
- Hard edges and geometric shapes

### Prompt Tips (for Text to Video)

**Effective keywords:**
- "soft light"
- "gentle"
- "flowing"
- "nature"
- "pastel colors"
- "dreamy"

**Example prompts:**
> "Cherry blossoms gently falling in a spring garden, soft morning light, peaceful atmosphere"

> "A calm lake at sunrise with mist rising from the water, soft pink and blue sky"

### Settings Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Motion Intensity | Low to Medium |
| Duration | 5-20 seconds works best |
| Best with | Nature, portraits, soft scenes |

## Style Applications

### Wedding Content
Perfect for save-the-dates, invitation videos, and reception slideshows. The romantic aesthetic enhances emotional moments.

### Nature Videos
Ideal for relaxation content, nature documentaries, and environmental pieces. Emphasizes the beauty of the natural world.

### Children's Content
Great for lullabies, storytime videos, and gentle educational content. The soft aesthetic is comforting.

### Wellness & Meditation
Perfect for meditation apps, yoga videos, and wellness brands. Creates a calming visual experience.

<Callout title="Pro Tip">
Images with soft, natural lighting work best. Golden hour photography translates beautifully into watercolor style.
</Callout>
