## Classical Masterpieces

The Oil Painting style transforms your content into moving works of art reminiscent of Van Gogh, Monet, and Rembrandt. Rich colors, visible brushstrokes, and dramatic lighting create timeless beauty.

## Visual Characteristics

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep, saturated hues
- **Accent:** Gold, amber, rich jewel tones
- **Base:** Warm or dramatic backgrounds

### Key Features
- Visible brushstroke textures
- Rich, saturated colors
- Dramatic chiaroscuro lighting
- Impasto effect (thick paint appearance)
- Classical composition
- Depth and dimension

### Mood
- Classical
- Dramatic
- Premium
- Timeless
- Artistic

## Best Use Cases

### Perfect For
- Portrait videos
- Premium brand content
- Artistic showcases
- Historical themes
- Museum and cultural pieces
- Fine art applications
- Dramatic presentations

### Examples

| Content Type | Result |
|-------------|--------|
| Portraits | Rembrandt-like lighting |
| Landscapes | Impressionist vistas |
| Still life | Classical composition |
| Scenes | Dramatic, painterly effects |

## Tips for Best Results

### Source Image Guidelines

**Ideal source images:**
- Portrait photography
- Landscapes with good lighting
- Images with clear subjects
- Photos with interesting light/shadow
- Rich color content

**Avoid:**
- Very flat lighting
- Overly busy compositions
- Low contrast images
- Poor quality or blurry photos

### Prompt Tips (for Text to Video)

**Effective keywords:**
- "dramatic lighting"
- "classical"
- "renaissance"
- "portrait"
- "golden hour"
- "majestic"

**Example prompts:**
> "A noble figure standing in a grand hall, dramatic side lighting, Renaissance atmosphere"

> "Rolling hills at sunset with a lone tree, golden light, impressionist landscape"

### Settings Recommendations

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Motion Intensity | Low to Medium |
| Duration | 5-20 seconds works best |
| Best with | Portraits, landscapes, still subjects |

## Style Applications

### Portrait Content
Perfect for professional headshots, artist portraits, and biographical videos. Elevates subjects with classical elegance.

### Premium Brands
Ideal for luxury products, heritage brands, and high-end marketing. Conveys quality and sophistication.

### Cultural Content
Great for museum videos, art documentaries, and historical presentations. Matches the subject matter.

### Dramatic Pieces
Perfect for emotional storytelling, dramatic reveals, and cinematic content. Adds gravitas and weight.

<Callout title="Pro Tip">
Images with directional lighting (light coming from one side) work exceptionally well, creating the dramatic chiaroscuro effect seen in classical paintings.
</Callout>
