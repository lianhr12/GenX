## Three Ways to Create

GenX offers three powerful methods to create stunning AI-generated videos. Each method is designed for different use cases and creative needs.

<Cards>

<Card href="/docs/features/image-to-video" title="Image to Video" icon={<ImageIcon />}>
Transform your static images into dynamic, animated videos. Perfect for bringing photos and artwork to life.
</Card>

<Card href="/docs/features/text-to-video" title="Text to Video" icon={<Type />}>
Generate videos purely from text descriptions. Ideal for creating content from your imagination.
</Card>

<Card href="/docs/features/reference-video" title="Reference Video" icon={<Video />}>
Use existing videos as style guides for consistent content creation. Great for brand consistency.
</Card>

</Cards>

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | Input | Best For | Creativity Level |
|---------|-------|----------|-----------------|
| Image to Video | 1-10 images | Transforming existing content | Guided by your images |
| Text to Video | Text prompt | Creating from imagination | Maximum creative freedom |
| Reference Video | Video + Image/Text | Maintaining consistency | Style-guided creation |

## Choosing the Right Feature

### Use Image to Video when:
- You have photos or artwork to transform
- You want to create photo montages
- You need specific visual content animated
- You're working with product images

### Use Text to Video when:
- You have a creative vision but no images
- You want to explore abstract concepts
- You need completely original content
- You're experimenting with ideas

### Use Reference Video when:
- You need consistent style across multiple videos
- You're building a brand visual identity
- You want to replicate a specific aesthetic
- You're creating a video series

## Getting Started

<Steps>

### Choose Your Method

Based on your content needs, select Image to Video, Text to Video, or Reference Video.

### Prepare Your Input

- **Image to Video:** Gather 1-10 high-quality images
- **Text to Video:** Write a descriptive prompt
- **Reference Video:** Have your reference video and source content ready

### Select Style and Settings

Choose an art style and configure duration, motion intensity, and other options.

### Generate and Download

Click generate, preview your video, and download when satisfied.

</Steps>
