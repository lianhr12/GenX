## Discover Your Artistic Voice

GenX offers five carefully curated artistic styles, each designed to transform your content into stunning visual art. This guide will help you understand each style and choose the perfect one for your project.

## Style Combinations

While each style stands on its own, consider these creative approaches:

1. **Transition between styles** - Start with one style and transition to another
2. **Match style to content** - Different scenes can use different styles
3. **Build a brand style** - Consistently use one style for brand recognition

## Tips for Each Style

### Cyberpunk Tips
- Use images with urban elements for best results
- Night scenes work particularly well
- Add contrast in your source images

### Watercolor Tips
- Soft, well-lit source images work best
- Nature and organic subjects shine
- Avoid high-contrast source material

### Oil Painting Tips
- Portraits and landscapes excel
- Rich colors in source images enhance results
- Works well with still or slow-moving content

### Anime Tips
- Clear subjects with defined edges work best
- Expressive scenes translate well
- Consider the storytelling aspect

### Fluid Art Tips
- Abstract or simple compositions work best
- Let the AI's creativity flow
- Great for backgrounds and overlays
