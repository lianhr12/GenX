## Welcome to GenX

GenX.art is an AI-powered artistic video creation platform that transforms your ideas into stunning visual content. Whether you're a content creator, social media manager, or creative professional, GenX makes video creation accessible to everyone.

## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up for a free account at [GenX.art](https://genx.art). New users receive **15 free credits** to start creating immediately.

### Step 2: Choose Your Creation Method

GenX offers three powerful ways to create videos:

<Cards>

<Card icon={<ImageIcon className="text-purple-300" />} title='Image to Video'>

Transform your static images into dynamic, artistic videos. Upload 1-10 images and watch them come to life.

</Card>

<Card icon={<Type className="text-blue-300" />} title='Text to Video'>

Describe your vision in words, and let AI generate stunning video content from your text prompts.

</Card>

<Card icon={<Video className="text-green-300" />} title='Reference Video'>

Use an existing video as a style reference to create new content with similar aesthetics.

### Step 3: Select Your Art Style

Choose from 5 stunning artistic styles:

- **Cyberpunk** - Neon-lit futuristic aesthetics
- **Watercolor** - Soft, flowing artistic effects
- **Oil Painting** - Rich, textured classical feel
- **Anime** - Japanese animation style
- **Fluid Art** - Mesmerizing liquid motion effects

### Step 4: Generate and Download

Click "Generate" and wait 15-60 seconds for your masterpiece. Preview, refine if needed, and download in 1080p quality.

## What You Can Create

- Product showcase videos
- Social media content
- Artistic expressions
- Marketing materials
- Educational content
- Personal creative projects

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use high-quality source images** - Better input means better output
2. **Be specific with text prompts** - Detailed descriptions yield better results
3. **Experiment with styles** - Each style produces unique effects
4. **Start with shorter videos** - Perfect your technique before longer projects
