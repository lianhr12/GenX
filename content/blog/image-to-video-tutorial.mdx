## Transform Still Images into Living Art

The Image to Video feature is GenX's core capability, allowing you to breathe life into your photographs and artwork. This comprehensive guide will walk you through every step of the process.

## What You'll Need

- **Images:** 1-10 high-quality images (JPG or PNG, max 10MB each)
- **Credits:** Each generation uses credits based on video length
- **A creative vision:** Know what style and mood you want to achieve

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Access the Creation Tool

Navigate to [Create â†’ Image to Video](/create/image) from the main menu or dashboard.

### Step 2: Upload Your Images

**Single Image Upload:**
- Click the upload area or drag and drop your image
- The image will be analyzed and prepared for transformation

**Multiple Images (2-10):**
- Upload images in the order you want them to appear
- GenX will create smooth transitions between images
- Perfect for creating photo montages or storytelling sequences

### Step 3: Configure Your Settings

**Art Style Selection:**

Choose from five artistic styles:
- Cyberpunk - Futuristic neon aesthetics
- Watercolor - Soft, flowing artistic effects
- Oil Painting - Rich, textured classical feel
- Anime - Japanese animation style
- Fluid Art - Mesmerizing liquid motion

**Video Duration:**
- Minimum: 3 seconds
- Maximum: 30 seconds
- Longer videos use more credits

**Additional Options:**
- Motion intensity (subtle to dramatic)
- Transition style (for multiple images)
- Output quality (1080p standard)

### Step 4: Preview and Generate

- Click "Generate" to start the AI processing
- Generation typically takes 15-60 seconds
- Watch the progress indicator

### Step 5: Review and Download

Once complete:
1. Preview your video in the built-in player
2. If satisfied, click "Download" for the full-quality file
3. If not satisfied, adjust settings and regenerate

## Pro Tips for Best Results

### Image Quality Matters

| Image Quality | Result Quality |
|--------------|----------------|
| High resolution (1080p+) | Excellent |
| Medium resolution (720p) | Good |
| Low resolution (under 480p) | May appear blurry |

### Optimal Image Characteristics

**Do:**
- Use well-lit images
- Include clear subjects
- Maintain consistent aspect ratios across multiple images
- Use images with interesting details

**Avoid:**
- Heavily compressed images
- Very dark or overexposed photos
- Extremely busy compositions
- Images with lots of text

### Style Matching

Different source images work better with different styles:

- **Portraits:** Oil Painting, Anime
- **Landscapes:** Watercolor, Oil Painting
- **Urban scenes:** Cyberpunk
- **Abstract images:** Fluid Art
- **Character shots:** Anime

## Multiple Image Strategies

### Creating a Narrative

Upload images that tell a story in sequence. GenX will create smooth transitions that enhance the narrative flow.

### Before/After Reveals

Upload the "before" image first, then the "after" image for a dramatic transformation reveal.

### Photo Montages

Perfect for:
- Event highlights
- Product showcases
- Portfolio presentations
- Memory compilations

## Troubleshooting

**Video looks different than expected:**
- Try a different art style
- Adjust motion intensity
- Use higher quality source images

**Transitions feel abrupt:**
- Ensure images have similar compositions
- Use images with complementary color palettes
- Consider the visual flow between images

**Generation takes too long:**
- Check your internet connection
- Try a shorter video duration
- Contact support if issues persist
