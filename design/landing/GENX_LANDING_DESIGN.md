# GenX.art Landing Page Design Spec

> Design Date: 2026-01-23  
> Product Position: AI Art Video Creation Platform  
> Slogan: Every Frame is Art  
> Target Market: Global (English-first)  
> Core Goal: User understands value and takes action within 5 seconds

---

## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Core Insight

Based on analysis of 8 competitors and product PRD, GenX.art's differentiation opportunity:

| Dimension | Competitors | GenX Opportunity |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| **Value Position** | Function-oriented (tools, model aggregation) | **Aesthetic-oriented** (art creation platform) |
| **Emotional Appeal** | Efficiency, speed, cost | **Artistic expression, creative freedom, aesthetic pursuit** |
| **Visual Presentation** | Tech dark / business white | **Artistic, colorful, gallery experience** |
| **User Mindset** | "Tool user" mentality | **"Artist/Creator" identity** |

### 1.2 Design Principles

1. **5-Second Rule**: Hero must deliver value within 5 seconds
2. **Emotion First**: Touch users' desire for beauty, not feature stacking
3. **Work as Proof**: Let AI-generated artwork speak for itself
4. **Minimal Path**: From landing to creation in 3 clicks or less

### 1.3 Core Value Proposition

**One-liner (English)**:
> Turn your photos into cinematic art

**Expanded version**:
> No skills needed. Upload a photo, pick an art style, get a stunning art video in 30 seconds.
